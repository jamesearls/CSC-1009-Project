About Us

What we do

We provide free information on cancers, local medical centres and medical professionals to give people the help they need to making their life with cancer less daunting and full of comfort and support. Our message board forums allow people to share their personal experiences and advice on coping with cancer. 

Our aim is to help people feel connected and supported during an extremely difficult, potentially isolating time in their lives. We conduct extensive research into different forms of cancer and this information is verified by our qualified and reputable doctors.  This information and research should be made freely available to all people, we believe, and we aim to deliver on this ethos.
